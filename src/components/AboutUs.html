About Us

My Search Engine is a student-built tool designed to deliver fast, accurate results for learning purposes. Created in 2025 by Team Searchers, it showcases modern web development techniques.

Our mission is to provide a simple, user-friendly search experience while demonstrating advanced UI/UX and responsive design. We believe learning should be easy and accessible for everyone.

Developed by Team Searchers as part of a frontend mini-project, this tool aims to make the internet a better learning resource.
